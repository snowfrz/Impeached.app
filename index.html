Impeached 2

Become President of the United States in this political satire strategy game

In a(nother) surprise victory, you have been elected President of the United States. You now wield the power to push your morals and values upon hundreds of millions of Americans. Will you be a benevolent leader or a ruthless dictator?

Make laws, fight wars, commit crimes and change the world, all on the taxpayer's dime! How long can you stay in power?

Use the Deck Editor to create & play custom decks, then save them as Virtual Cartridge images to your camera roll. You can share your Virtual Cartridges with your friends by DM or post them publicly on social media!

Note: This game is political satire, and is not meant to be factually accurate. If Impeached 2 isn't funny enough for you, check out the Deck Editor!

I have taken the feedback received by Impeached: Be The President constructively, and have changed many things to make the gameplay experience better. Enjoy!


Impeached 2 includes:

  • 200+ scenario cards, and a built-in Deck Editor to make and play your own custom cards
  • 40+ endings with pixel art graphics. Try to get them all!
  • Virtual Cartridges to let you share your custom decks with friends and post them directly to social media!
  • Both chambers of Congress, with bribery & accurate election cycles
  • Lots of crime to commit, but avoid being caught!
  • Economic "features" like taxes & inflation
  • Much, much more!

Will you be a champion of the Constitution, a self-serving politician or a power-hungry tyrant? The choice is yours!
